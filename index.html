About Team Hiraya

Team Hiraya is a small, aspiring indie game developer inspired by Team Cherry, Polychroma Games, and Mojang. We are a group of 1st-year Computer Science students at Pamantasan ng Cabuyao and there are 5 members in our team:

  • Kenneth – Manager / Head
  • Ian Daniel – Programmer
  • Paz – Programmer
  • Jenny Losamia – Game Designer
  • Kyle Acosta – Game Designer

We are currently developing a game called Raya, a 2D side-scroller inspired by Filipino folklore. Players will control a girl who fights monsters in the Philippines. This game is a work in progress and our first project.

The team name "Hiraya" represents freedom, creativity, and hope. We are passionate game developers, eager to grow, and invite everyone to join and support our game development journey.
