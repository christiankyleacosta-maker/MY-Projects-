College of Computing Studies
School department

The College of Computing Studies (CCS) is dedicated to developing highly skilled and innovative professionals in the fields of Computer Science and Information Technology. Our department provides students with a strong foundation in computing principles, programming, systems development, and modern technologies that drive today’s digital world.

At CCS, we combine academic excellence with hands-on learning experiences to prepare students for real-world challenges in the technology industry. Through updated curricula, experienced faculty members, and modern laboratories, we ensure that students gain the knowledge, skills, and creativity needed to succeed in rapidly evolving technological environments.

More About the Department of CCS

Vision

A premier institution of higher learning in Region IV, developing globally competitive and value laden professionals and leaders intrumental to community development and nation building.

Mission

As an institution of higher learning, PnC is committed to equip individuals with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to achieve their professional goals & provide leadership and service for national development.

Core Values

History

Ang Pamantasan ng Cabuyao (PnC) sa Banay-Banay, Lungsod ng Cabuyao, Laguna, ay nagsimula bilang Cabuyao Community College na itinatag noong 1993 at nagtapos noong Marso 31, 2003. Opisyal itong nagsimulang mag-alok ng mga kurso sa Business Administration, Information Technology, Education, Nursing, at Midwifery noong Hunyo 2003.

Project One Percent

About this Event
✨ Build with vision. Bridge the future. ✨ Every expert was once a beginner. Every great programmer started with a single line of code. 💻 And sometimes, all it takes is just 1% improvement every day to build something extraordinary.
The Association of Computer Science Students (ACSS) proudly presents the first session of Project One Percent, a peer tutoring initiative designed to help students strengthen their programming foundations and grow their skills step by step. 📈
In this session, we will explore the basics of Java Fundamentals and Loop Structures—an essential concept that allows programs to repeat tasks efficiently and solve problems in smarter ways. 🔁 Whether you're just starting your programming journey or looking to reinforce your understanding, this session will guide you through the core ideas you need to build stronger coding logic.
💡 This is your chance to:
🔹 Understand the basics of Java programming
🔹 Learn how loops make coding more efficient
🔹 Strengthen your problem-solving and logical thinking
🔹 Connect and learn with fellow Computer Science students
Remember, growth does not happen overnight—but 1% progress each day leads to remarkable results over time. 🌱
📚 Topic: Java Fundamentals – Loop Structures
📅 Date: Sunday, March 15
⏰ Time: 3:00 PM
💻 Platform: Online (Google Meet)
📢 This session is open for all Computer Science students, but it is highly recommended for first-year students who want to review and better understand Java loops.
