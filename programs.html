CCS Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
Focused on Software Development and Programming

The Computer Science program focuses on programming, algorithms, software development, and problem-solving. Students learn how to design and build applications, websites, and computer systems that solve real-world problems.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
Managing and Supporting Digital Systems

The Information Technology program focuses on networking, system administration, cybersecurity, and IT support. Students learn how to manage computer systems, networks, and digital infrastructure used in organizations.

Computer Science Specializations

Software Development

Build applications using programming languages and modern development tools.

Web Development

Create websites and web apps with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks.

App Development

Build mobile and desktop apps for Android, iOS, and Windows.

Information Technology Specializations

Cybersecurity

Protect systems and networks from cyber threats and attacks.

Networking

Learn about computer networks, servers, routing, and infrastructure.

IT Support

Maintain systems, troubleshoot problems, and support IT operations.
